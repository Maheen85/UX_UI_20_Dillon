Project Overview


Problem


We were inspired by Paw Fund's mission and the incredible work they do in "helping people help their pets". Paw fund, while having a great purpose,lacks an up to date website.
We wanted to help create a more user-friendly and visually compelling online presence for Paw Fund.

The Solution


We will redesign PAW Fund's website to be a professional and appealing platform that provides resources for new and existing pet owners in a more categorized and easily navigable way.
This redesign will boost the draw of Paw Fund's site, and will allow users to easily access the resources they desire.

User Flow


Through our previous steps we were able to sort out a flushed out user flow that would highlight and convey the proper tasks to help with the overall design overhaul.

Wireframing

For our wireframing process we each individually came up with our own wireframes and ideas. After this we met together as a group and talked about our designs and ideas. Finally we worked together to combine our designs and ideas into one flushed out wireframe.

Low-Fi Prototype

After flushing out our wireframe we continued on to the low-fi. We wanted to make sure that we focused on our priorities through this process and also follow the user flow.

Usability Testing

For our final round of testing before the high-fi process begins we wanted to see if our user's could complete our three tasks without running into any problems. Any problems that may arise helps us further flush out our process to make sure we deliver the best possible results.
